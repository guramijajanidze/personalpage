Projects

EaP Civil Society Fellowship 2023

EaP Civil Society Fellowship 2022-2023 was a fellowship project funded by the European Union within the framework of the Eastern Partnership Civil Society Facility work. As a result I received an individual grant - with the amoung of 5000 EUROs to carry out the project under the name- Active Citizen – The Key Pillar of Local Democracy.

Project Budget: 5000 EUR

US Embassy Alumni Grant [PAS] 2022

With the support of US Embassy Alumni Grant [PAS] in 2022 I secured the funding for the project - Strong Self-Government – The Cornerstone of Democracy. The project was carried out under the mandate of CSO - Europe-Georgia Institute. It was based on the idea of popularization of floor-game - "Archevneti", originally Swedish game translated into Georgian and adjusted to Georgian context by Europe-Georgia Institute in 2021.

Project Budget: 9590 USD

GMF Empowering Leaders during the times of Pandemic 2021

The project School of Local Democracy aimed to provide the young leaders from Gori municipality with the opportunity to learn about the local civil participation mechanisms and increase their awareness about the competences and functioning of local self-government bodies, media and civil-society organizations. Respectively all three components of the project were related to this objective.

Project Budget: 2500 EUR
